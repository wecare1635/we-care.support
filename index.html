Mission

To empower families by providing compassionate and reliable care for the elderly in Bangladesh, ensuring they receive the health support they need while fostering peace of mind for their loved ones living abroad.

Purpose

WeCare is dedicated to bridging the gap between families and their elderly loved ones. Our platform connects families living overseas with trusted helpers in Bangladesh, facilitating essential healthcare services. We aim to enhance the well-being of older adults by making healthcare access easier, fostering independence, and ensuring families stay connected and involved in their care.

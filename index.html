Connecting Families Abroad with Trusted Caregivers in Bangladesh

Mission

To empower families by providing compassionate and reliable care for the elderly in Bangladesh, ensuring they receive the health support they need while fostering peace of mind for their loved ones living abroad.

Purpose

WeCare is dedicated to bridging the gap between families and their elderly loved ones. Our platform connects families living overseas with trusted helpers in Bangladesh, facilitating essential healthcare services. We aim to enhance the well-being of older adults by making healthcare access easier, fostering independence, and ensuring families stay connected and involved in their care.

How to Avail Our Service

Step 1: Pickup or Meet

Pickup from the patient’s location or meet at a safe location before visiting the WeCare office.

Step 2: Pickup and Transport

Our employee assists the patient into the vehicle and ensures comfort and safety during transport.

Step 3: Support at Appointment

The employee stays with the patient, communicates with the doctor, and notes important instructions.

Step 4: Pharmacy Stop

Stop at the pharmacy to collect prescribed medications before heading home.

Step 5: Drop-off at Home

Ensure the patient is comfortably settled at home and review post-visit instructions if needed.

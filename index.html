Connecting Families Abroad with Trusted Caregivers in Bangladesh

Mission

To empower families by providing compassionate and reliable care for the elderly in Bangladesh, ensuring they receive the health support they need while fostering peace of mind for their loved ones living abroad.

Purpose

WeCare is dedicated to bridging the gap between families and their elderly loved ones. Our platform connects young people living overseas with trusted human helpers in Bangladesh, facilitating essential healthcare services. We aim to enhance the well-being of older adults by making healthcare access easier, fostering independence, and ensuring that families stay connected and involved in their care.

Our Services

  • Accompanying elderly to:
    • Doctors Chamber
    • Diagnostic Test Center
    • Pharmacy
  • Transport Management
  • Patient Pickup and Drop-off
  • Doctors Appointment Oversight
  • Live Status Update During Service
  • Note Taking During Doctor's Visit

How to Avail Our Service

Call us or email us to get started. Here's how it works:

Contact Established ↓ Date Fixed ↓ Employee Meets Patient at Safe Location ↓ Pickup and Transport to Doctor's Office ↓ Stay with Patient, Take Notes ↓ Drop-off at Home or Preferred Location ↓ Buy Medicine from Pharmacy if Required
